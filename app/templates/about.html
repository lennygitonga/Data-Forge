Our Story

We believe data should work for people, not the other way around.

Data-Forge was built by a small team tired of copying and pasting information from websites. We decided to build the tool we always wished existed.

How We Started

Born out of frustration. Built with purpose.

In 2023, our founders were spending hours every week manually collecting data from competitor websites, news sources, and market feeds. Copy. Paste. Format. Repeat. It was exhausting and error-prone.

So we built Data-Forge — a platform where you simply name a website, and intelligent automation handles everything else. Within months, what used to take hours now takes seconds.
